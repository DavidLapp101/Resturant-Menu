About Us

We are a off brand uber eats but better, we've noticed that uber eats was charging to much, so as any good start up company we plan to destroy them with our prices, by charging even more. Ubers delivery fee is $10 and our fee is $50, their prices are based on the food companys prices, but we take their price and raise it by 100% that way you pay more money, since we feel the more it cost the better it is. You might be eating fast food but the charge on your bill, will be as much as a gourmet meal at Gordon Ramsays.
